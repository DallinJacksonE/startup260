About
My Origin

At the end of 2019, my family was watching the Madolorian when we, with everyone else, were surprised with the appearance of baby yoda. I had been crocheting for a few years, and decided to make a few baby yodas. They were very popular, and from word of mouth people started asking me to make stuffed animals I had crocheted. I began an Instagram page, and with lots of work and practice, started selling my creations. I've expanded to hats, bags, and blankets, but my most popular items are my plushies. Here in 2024, you can find a plethora of hand-made creations here online, on Instagram, or at fairs in the Northern Colorado area.
