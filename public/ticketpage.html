Placing an Order

Feel free to chat with me about any questions about available colors or options before placing your order.

If you want to make small changes to the item (color, size, number of spots, etc.), or have any comments about the item, please write it in the comments box.

If you want to make a custom order, please email Kaylie at kayliescreations30@gmail.com.

If you want to make an order that is more than 6 items, please email Kaylie at kayliescreations30@gmail.com.

Orders can take up to 14 days to ship, so plan with a maximum of 19 days from purchase to delivery.

The chat is with the real Kaylie, not a bot, so response times won't be immediate. Feel free to email Kaylie with any more urgent or detailed matters.
